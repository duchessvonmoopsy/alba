My Background
Born in Ponte San Pietro (BG), Italy, in 1994, I moved to Boston with my family in 2004 at the age of 10 years old. As soon as we moved to the United States, my father looked to preserve the Italian language and culture in our family by continuing to speak to me and my brother in Italian, cooking Italian dishes, and going to authentic Italian Boston restaurants, such as Eataly and the now unfortunately closed Erbaluce. I have found that being bilingual has helped me navigate society and connecting with people on a deeper level by understanding them more and having an idea of where they are coming from. This is the reason why I love teaching Italian to people who would like to learn; it brings to awareness an experience of life that would otherwise be unknown.
